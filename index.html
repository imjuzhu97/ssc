How It Works

Everything is designed so the room keeps moving and the conversations have somewhere to go.

01

Before the night

We send a quick pre-survey to understand your preferences and curate the room before you arrive.

02

Dinner

You start at a table of four, rotate after each course, and meet new faces without forcing the moment.

03

The afters

Fill out a Cupid Card with your crush. If the interest is mutual, we make the intro after dinner.
